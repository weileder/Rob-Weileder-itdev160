Home renovation by repurposing discarded items.

In the past few years, I have come to realize that we are quickly becoming a disposible society.
People would rather replace old worn out items around their homes with newly manufactured items. They throw away doors, hardware, lighting fixtures, and furniture because it no longer suits their moods or needs. There are alternatives that are much better for our planet and much more satisfying.

There are lots of items that can be reused.

There are many items that can be repurposed into other things or even just repainted to suit a new color scheme. Below is a list of some household items that can be repurposed in one way or another.

  • Doors, both interior and exterior
  • Lighting fixtures
  • Window sashes
  • Tables
  • Furniture
  • Dishes
  • Flower pots

Doors can be refinished and have glass panels installed to use as pantry or powder room doors. Lighting fixtures can be repainted to go with a new color scheme. Old windows sashes can be hung in a random pattern on a wall to create an artistic feel. The wood from tables can be used to make smaller decorative items, such as frames and shelves. Worn furniture can be recovered and even have new padding put in. Dishes can be used to create multi-level desert plates by gluing coffee or tea cups in between plates. Flower pots can be painted and used to hold pencils or charging cords and adapters.
